Design Challenge

To identify the challenge at hand, IDEO's Human Centered Design questionnaire was followed.

What is the problem you’re trying to solve?

Creating opportunities for Christian outreach and connections through art.

Framing the design question.

How would a dedicated website be useful in reaching out and sharing information with the community?

What's the ultimate impact you're trying to have?

Inform people and encourage people to attend and participate in the ministry’s art functions.

Finding possible solutions.

  • Create a separate, dedicated website for the ministry.
  • Feature artist’s work to spark interest.
  • Feature a list of upcoming events with relevant detail.
  • Provide contact information.
  • Include an “About Us” page.

Context and constraints.

Most of the users would be Japanese, and I don’t speak that language. Also, the ministry is run by missionaries, so people in the United States may be interested in using the website to keep up with the missionaries they support. Two sets of users may need to be accounted for and catered to in different circumstances.

Modify the original question.

How would a dedicated website be effective in striking people’s interest while informing them and inviting them to a variety of artistic functions?
